Ramen

Description

Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup. It consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a meat- or (occasionally) fish-based broth, often flavored with soy sauce or miso, and uses toppings such as sliced pork

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of ramen noodles
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 green onions
  • 1/2 cup of corn
  • 1/2 cup of bamboo shoots
  • 1/2 cup of mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup of bean sprouts
  • 1/2 cup of sliced pork
  • 1/2 cup of spinach
  • 1/2 cup of seaweed

Steps

  1. Boil the eggs
  2. Boil the noodles
  3. Prepare the toppings
  4. Assemble the ramen
